Why does it have to be a secret?

Some things just aren't mean to be shared in the open. Others shouldn't be hidden from view. My mom makes it seem like I'm insane for liking what I do, am I insane or is she just intolerant. I make myself into something I'm not, but I know that I'm not what I make myself. I feel four, but I'm not, you know? I don't push this on others, I tell them because I think they might be able to help make me feel better. I know there's at least one person that's interested in this website, but I don't think he'll ever find this. If you did somehow make it here, hi Cameron.

Back when the r/TBDL subreddit existed I'd point my friends to the Wikipedia article on paraphilic infantilism, now I know better. The subreddit was a mistake, I'm glad it's gone. I tried my hardest to keep the ship sailing while I was there, but eventually I saw its flaws. It's not a fetish, it's how I cope with stress. It's how I express love. There's so much more to being an actual AB/DL than the internet thinks. I hate people who make it sexual, they have no right to. Look, I'm just trying to be a normal person out here. People like Riley Kilo make people like me look bad. He makes me sick, no sane AB/DL should identify with, or even relate to him.
